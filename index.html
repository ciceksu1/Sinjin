More Questions?

  • What is the opportunity and what is Experience City?

    Your Employee Experience strategy success is enabled by our immersive virtual platform: Experience City. Our unique, tailored concept connects your people to your needs, unlocking the wisdom of your organization. Harnessing vital people insights, we help you build a productive, capability-driven ‘experience ecosystem’, with the power to solve your most critical problems.

  • How does Kallisto enable companies to unlock seamless experiences for their people?

    We provide a world-leading platform and framework to turn REAL insights into action and then, embed the right solutions for your organisation. We have the platform, tools and experience to help you ask the vital questions. We bring an internal research tool to connect your people directly with your challenges and then amplify those insights to solve the right problems for them.

  • Why is Kallisto’s platform so pioneering in the Employee Experience (EX) space?

    Our unique platform makes it fun and rewarding to engage with and build EX design capability from our research and experience design tools. Gamified, playful themes blur the lines between work and play, with rich data and KPI outputs. We build with you a dedicated, highly diverse research demographic community, committed to contributing their local knowledge to fixing global problems.

    Collaboratively validate your audience, understand their needs, explore the best solution for them and then bring it to life - all within our disruptive, world leading, tried and tested virtual environment.

  • How does Kallisto support transformational change?

    Over many years, we have enabled global organizations to lead transformations to success through the highest levels of employee experience engagement. We know how hard it is to learn each other's challenges, needs, goals and pain points, without the right research insights. Implementation risk is one of the biggest issues facing transformation, where audiences are routinely unprepared for change. Not being able to find the right information when you need it, or the right people to share their insights on a new initiative, can be an avoidably expensive setback.

  • Why is Kallisto's experience design framework so valuable?

    With Design Thinking inspired tools and activities, our Experience 4D Framework pinpoints our audience, their needs and the exact problem they're trying to solve. We then explore lots of potential solutions. We zoom in to focus on the best one. And that’s the one we embed, to de-risk the launch of your service, campaign or transformation. Tried and tested Experience Design – at your fingertips.
